Toast

Description

A great, simple and fast breakfast item to start the day or at anytime that serves 1 person.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (any type)
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Place the bread in the toaster and leave the timer for 3 minutes (this may need to be adjusted depending on the power of your toaster).
  2. Place a bit of butter on both sides and serve.
